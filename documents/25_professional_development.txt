# Professional Development

## Professional Development

The purpose of this series is to help international students of all majors and degree levels develop the professional skills required in today's job market, explore the employment opportunities available to international students while a Georgia Tech student and after graduation, determine how international students can take advantage of F-1/J-1 employment benefits and learn about other employment-based visa categories.
